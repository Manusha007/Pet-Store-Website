Cutest Dog Breeds in the World

Pembroke Welsh Corgi

The Pembroke Welsh Corgi is an active and intelligent dog breed, originally bred for the herds of cattle , sheep and horses. Pembrokes are ideal for children and other animals, easy to train and keen to know, and are available in four different colors and markings for the hair. Also if they are pure bred dogs, they can be placed in shelters or rescue organisations. Remember to adopt! Remember to adopt! When you want to take a dog home, don't shop. Corgis will work in almost any home, be it an apartment or a big house with a yard, adaptable and caring to the entire family. But for a small dog they have a little room. You will need plenty of walks and fun games. How quickly those pups with short legs can run, you may be shocked! The Pembroke is an ideal family partner particularly for inexperienced animal parents and humans who can meet the race 's needs.

Golden Retriever

One of the most common dog breeds in the United States is the Golden Retriever. The friendly and compassionate nature of the species makes them big family animals and makes them very good working dogs. Hunting, tracking, sniffing off contraband for compliance, and as therapies and dogs for welfare are outstanding. Golden Retrievers They are also normal and strong in dog sports such as agility and competition. they are also natural athletes. Such dogs can be raised and enjoyed relatively easily in any home and family. With baby, they 're nice and their people are very protective. If you want a faithful, caring and intelligent friend, consider having one of them.

Yorkshire Terrier

Yorkshire Terrier makes a feisty, but caring pet, compact but broad with personality. The "Yorkie" has won many fans by its loyalty to its parents, its stylish appearance and its suitability for apartment living, the most famous toy dog breed in the USA.
