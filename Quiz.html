Are You Guys Ready For This ???

Let's Start This...

The only thing that is more interesting than any step made by our dog is their past and the facts about them. You may think you are an expert on dogs, but how much do you really know? With this quiz challenge your canine expertise and find yourself as professional as your vet! We are going to ask you a few well-known and unknown dog facts during this dog trivia quiz. Your dog experience will be checked from the oldest race in the world to the most powerful race. Take a while to sit and wait until you answer our request. You wouldn't like an quick answer because you're just looking forward to going to the park. Will you do as you say, or do you have to be sent back to the school of obedience?  Answer our trivia questions and find out!

1.Which dog breed is the favourite of The Queen of England?


    Pembroke Welsh Corgi

2.What breed is Scooby Doo?




    Great Dane

3.How old was the World’s oldest dog when he died?




    29

4.Which breed of dog has a black tongue?

    Chow Chow


5.How many teeth does a fully grown adult dog have?



    42

6.What is the name of the smallest dog breed?


    Chihuahua

7.Which medical condition affects dogs the most?

    Obesity


8.Which breed is considered the world's rarest?



    Cesky terrier

9.Which human organ do dogs lack?


    Appendix

10.Which dog breed has six toes on each paw?




    Norwegian Lundehund
